At a high level it cuts out 30-45 minutes a day for the person managing the daily screeners starting with email reminders, filtering, doing V-lookups, and other related tasks.
When it comes to the employees completing the screener it cuts out 30 seconds to a minute when filling out each screener because it auto fills their employee id (if known), reduces questions (and errors), and gives helpful guidance on known sticking points.

The screener human resources administrator position can be filled in with minimal training.
If you compare it to a very manual excel survey monkey data dump that adds up not only in employee happines, but overall business value.
No one planned for COVID 19 everyone had to find ways to manage the risks and associated costs.
The screener has been instrumental in lowering burden for the entire organization.
